Research & Equity Group

We are a cross-disciplinary group of PhD student who recognize how social contexts and locations have deepened existing academic inequalities and created new challenges. To help address at least some of these problem, we have come together to build a network that connects those who need academic support with those who can offer it. Together, we aim to collaboratively develop ideas, research designs, and identify the right skills to advance your work. We also host workshops on research methods and professional development over the summer for master's and PhD students, with undergraduates interested in research also welcome. If you're interested in joining as a mentor or participant, please feel free to reach out.
